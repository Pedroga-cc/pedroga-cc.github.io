Undergraduate course for second-year Physics and Astronomy students covering the mathematical tools needed for the study of various Physical Phenomena, like Electromagnetism, Thermodynamics or Fluid Mechanics. The course is divided into two modules and eight topics:

Module 1: Linear Algebra
======

* Lineal Algebra

* Multivariable Differential Calculus

* Multivariable Integral Calculus

* Curvilineal Coordinate Systems

* Vector Calculus

Module 2: Mathematical Series
======

* Numerical Series

* Functional Series and Taylor Series

* Fourier Series
