Undergraduate course for third-year Physics students covering the mathematical tools needed for the study of Quantum Mechanics. The course is divided into two modules and four topics:

Module 1: Linear Algebra
======

* Lineal Spaces

* Operator Theory

Module 2: Introduction to Group Theory
======

* Group Theory

* Representation Theory

In this occasion, the classes had to be made via online meetings, as a consequence of the COVID-19 Pandemic.
